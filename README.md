## 🎯 Why This Roadmap?

Cybersecurity is one of the fastest-growing fields, but the learning path can be overwhelming. This roadmap provides a **structured, step-by-step approach** to becoming a cybersecurity professional.

### What Makes This Roadmap Different?

✅ **Practical & Hands-On** - Not just theory, but real tools and techniques  
✅ **Industry-Aligned** - Covers what employers actually look for  
✅ **Self-Paced** - Flexible timeline for beginners and fast learners  
✅ **Free Resources** - All tools and platforms are free or have free tiers
